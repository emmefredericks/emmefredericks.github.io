    • Faygo Rebrand

    Prompt: Update the branding for Faygo Beverages.

    Faygo is a beverage company made in Detroit with over 30 flavors. The company currently uses bright colors and a funky font to brand the carbonated drinks. I wanted to keep the fun nature of the brand with an updated twist. I used an 80s color scheme and kept bits of the original font to create a more modern version of the brand.

    I included four new pop labels, an in-store display, and an Instagram post with the new branding.
