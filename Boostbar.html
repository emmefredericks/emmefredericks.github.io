    Boost Bar Brand Identity

    I created a brand identity for a fictional smoothie and juice shop called "Boost Bar". I used a mix of loud and bright colors with simple sans serif fonts to come across playful and inviting. To avoid a busy looking logo, I used a simple circle and stuck with two complementary colors to create a recognizable pop. I also included a preview of the Boost Bar app which provides an example of the shop's rewards program.
