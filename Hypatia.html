    Prompt: Design a poster that demonstrates the range of your chosen typeface. Highlight its style and unique characteristics. Use CMYK color and show at least 4 variants the typeface can exhibit.

    I chose Hypatia Sans Pro as my font. It demonstrates the letters in both upper and lowercase, showcases 7 variants, and provides a brief history of the font. I used some geometrical elements to add contrast to the text and I used different opacities for style.
