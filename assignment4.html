Challenge 5: Accessibility Concerns

When viewing my website there are several challenges that might be arise for a viewer. First, in challenge 2, there is no alternate text that describes the chess board for visually impaired viewers. In order to solve this issue I would add a text description of the image or somehow try and incorporate a text to speak feature to describe the board. Another issue that could be presented is that the colors in challenge 2 and 4 may be difficult to process. To fix this I could change them to be meet color contrast standards and ratios. Hopefully these two adjustments along with others will improve my websites accessibility.
