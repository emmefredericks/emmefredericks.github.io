    Underdog Branding

    Prompt: Design a playful and luxury dog brand.

    Underdog is a high end dog product line that would ideally be sold at Nordstrom. The target consumer is a dog-obsessed Millennial who values high quality products that are aesthetically pleasing. The line currently includes a poop pale, custom balls, a leash, and treats.
